## Section 3: Locked falsifiable laboratory predictions

### 3.1 Born-rule modification near nuclear-isomer source

**Specification:** Double-slit electron-beam interferometry near a 1 mg ¹⁷⁸ᵐ²Hf source at 5 cm distance.

**Predicted signal:** Born-rule modification of magnitude **13.6 ± 5 ppm** at d = 5 cm, scaling as 1/d² with characteristic length ~1 m.

**Predicted control behavior:** Zero modification (within sensitivity) when source is replaced with ground-state ¹⁷⁸Hf at same mass and distance.

### 3.2 Tsirelson-bound enhancement near matter-channel sources

**Specification:** CHSH Bell-test inequality violation in proximity (~5 cm) to strong matter-channel source.

**Predicted signal:** S_observed = 2√2 × (1 + 0.12 × δβ/β\_\*), exceeding standard Tsirelson bound by up to 1.2% at modest excitation, up to ~12% at saturation.

### 3.3 Gravitational slow-light analog

**Predicted signal:** v_g/c ≈ 0.89 at moderate field amplitude (δχ/χ_* = 0.5); approaches v_g/c → 0 at saturation.

### 3.4 Excess time dilation in optical-lattice clocks

**Specification:** Optical-lattice clock at 5 cm from 1 mg ¹⁷⁸ᵐ²Hf source.

**Predicted signal:** Anomalous fractional clock-rate modification of approximately **10⁻⁹** at the 5 cm distance, exceeding gravitational-redshift expectation. Detectable with current optical-lattice clock sensitivity (~10⁻¹⁸ fractional precision).


## Section 4: Locked falsifiable cosmological predictions

### 4.1 DESI dark-energy equation of state

**Locked claim:** Continued DESI Year 5+ data will refine w₀ toward -0.93401, *not* toward -1, *not* toward -0.85 or below.

**Falsification criterion:** w₀ converging on |w₀ + 1| < 0.01 (i.e., w₀ → -1 at sub-percent precision) would falsify Section 2.3.

### 4.2 Euclid / Roman dark matter substructure

**Locked claim:** High-resolution dark-matter clustering measurements will reveal a subdominant warm/sterile component at fraction **15% (±10%) of total dark matter**.

**Falsification criterion:** Pure cold-dark-matter behavior with no warm/sterile component above ~1% fraction would falsify Section 2.9.

### 4.3 Hubble tension persistence

**Locked claim:** The H₀ tension between local-distance-ladder and CMB-anchored measurements persists at the 6-12% level, not resolved by methodological refinement.

**Falsification criterion:** Tension converging on sub-percent agreement would falsify Section 2.4. Tension growing beyond 15% would also falsify (the framework predicts a specific magnitude, not unbounded growth).

### 4.4 Cosmological-density precision matches

**Locked claim:** Continued cosmological surveys (Stage IV: Euclid, Roman, LSST, CMB-S4) will refine measurements of Ω_b, Ω_m, Ω_DE toward the values in Section 2.2 within recursion noise floor.

**Falsification criterion:** Any of Ω_b, Ω_m, Ω_DE drifting beyond 5% from UM predictions in refined measurements would falsify Section 2.2.
